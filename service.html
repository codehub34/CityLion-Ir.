About Company

Professional and Expert Energy Supplier

We import and distribute petroleum products and general merchandise across Liberia, with a growing focus on affordability and sustainability. City Lion is a Liberian-owned enterprise that imports refined petroleum products and general merchandise. We have three office locations: (1) Supermarket, Gardnerville, Japan Freeway, Monrovia (headquarters) (2) BMC Building, Opposite UBA Bank, Freeport, Bushrod Island, Monrovia and (3) Opp Liberia Water & Sewer, Gurley & Front Streets, Central Monrovia, Liberia.

Meet Our CEO

Omaru Sheriff is a Liberian businessman from Lofa County and co-founder of S & Sow, a forex and money remittance, City Life Incorporated, City Life Mineral Water and Child Life Incorporated. He is passionate about using business to change lives in Liberia. He was motivated to enter the petroleum industry to meet the growing petrol demand as Total Energies had divested her assets, and the petrol shortage was becoming frequent.
